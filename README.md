# Audio Engineering Workflows

This repository contains practical notes, workflows, and examples related to voice editing, cleanup, and audio preparation for spoken-word and music projects.

The focus is on clarity, consistency, and performance, especially for voice-based content such as podcasts, interviews, and narration.

## What you'll find here
- Voice cleanup and editing workflows
- Session organization and delivery practices
- Notes on preparing audio for voice-based production
- Practical production tips from real-world projects
